Data Deletion Instructions

If you signed in to Aura using Facebook, you can request that we delete all personal data associated with your account at any time. Here's how:

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Email us directly at auracompanionlc@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address or Facebook account you used to sign in.
  2. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation once your data has been permanently deleted.
  3. You can also remove Aura's access to your Facebook account at any time by going to Facebook Settings → Security and Login → Apps and Websites and removing Aura from the list.

What Gets Deleted

Upon a deletion request, we permanently remove:

  1. Your account profile and all associated data
  2. All conversation history with your Aura companion
  3. Any preferences, memory, or personalization data
  4. Your email address and any other identifying information received from Facebook

Contact

For any questions about data deletion, contact us at auracompanionlc@gmail.com.
